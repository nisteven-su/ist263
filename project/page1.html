The High Price of Fast Fashion

Welcome to the Eco Edit . We provide information and tips on how to live life sustainably as a college student. Fast fashion’s biggest consumer is students enrolled in college. As a student myself, I know of the struggles of buying ethically and consciously on a lower budget. However, this website aims to educate and offer insight on how to buy sustainably. Every consumer has a role in making ethical decisions for our environment. Your journey starts here….

About Me:

I am Nicole Stevens and a senior at Syracuse University. I will be graduating in May 2021 with an undergraduate degree in Policy Studies and a minor in Information Management and Technology. Over my time at Syracuse University, I discovered my growing interest in entering the retail world. I have completed internships at PVH Corp and Ted Baker. From these experiences, I have gained insights on how clothing is manufactured. Therefore, I have gained an understanding of how fast fashion has caused detrimental effects on our planet. In order to combat these growing issues, I believe that investing in “slow” fashion is beneficial to the consumer and the environment.
